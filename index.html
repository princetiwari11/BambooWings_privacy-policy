Privacy Policy for Bamboo Wings

Last updated: November 2025

Bamboo Wings is an offline mobile game designed for entertainment. This Privacy Policy explains that the game does not collect, store, or share any user data.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Bamboo Wings does not collect any personal or non-personal information. The game does not gather:

  • Name, email, phone number
  • Device identifiers
  • Gameplay analytics
  • Location data
  • Contacts or personal files
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Cookies or tracking data

2. No Third-Party Services

The game does not use any third-party tools or SDKs, such as:

  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • Crash reporting services
  • Social login systems

No data is sent outside the game.

3. Children's Privacy

Bamboo Wings is suitable for all ages and does not collect any information from children, in accordance with global child-privacy regulations.

4. Permissions

The game does not request any permissions that access personal information, files, contacts, or device identifiers. Bamboo Wings runs entirely offline.

5. Security

Since no information is collected or stored, no personal data can be lost, exposed, or misused.

6. Changes to This Policy

If future updates introduce features that require data usage, this Privacy Policy will be updated before any changes take effect.

7. Contact

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you may reach out through the official GitHub repository’s Issues section.
